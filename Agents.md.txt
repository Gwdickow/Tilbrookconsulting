You are helping build a Google Apps Script workflow for Tilbrook Consulting.

Project goal:
Build a Google Sheets-driven customer brief generator.

Primary workflow:
- Read customer/prospect rows from Google Sheets.
- Only process rows where Status = "Ready".
- Generate a one-page Growth Opportunity Snapshot.
- Create a Google Doc.
- Write the Google Doc link back to the sheet.
- Optionally export a PDF.
- Optionally create a Gmail draft.
- Never automatically send emails.
- Never overwrite existing briefs unless explicitly instructed.

Tone for generated briefs:
Consultative, practical, respectful, specific, and not overly salesy.

Audience:
Small business owners and managers who may be interested in growth, AI visibility, customer connection strategies, and local business development.
